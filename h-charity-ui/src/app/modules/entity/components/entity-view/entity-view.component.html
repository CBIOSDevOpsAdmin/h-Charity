About {{entity && entity.name}}

    The Madeena Masjid Reathibowli, located near pillar no. 52 in Dilshad Nagar Colony, stands 
    as a significant place of worship for the local Muslim community. While the exact date of 
    its construction remains unknown, it has long been a spiritual hub where residents gather 
    to offer their daily prayers. Alhamdulillah, the masjid facilitates the five daily prayers, 
    bringing together the faithful in devotion and community spirit. During the holy month of 
    Ramadan, the masjid holds special Taraweeh prayers, creating an atmosphere of heightened 
    religious observance and unity. The Madeena Masjid continues to play a crucial role in the 
    religious life of Dilshad Nagar Colony, fostering a sense of peace and belonging among its 
    worshippers.

              WHO IS INCHARGE OF THE FUNDS?

The Masjid committee that is approved by the T.S. Waqf Board comprises of 
well qualified and dedicated people working selflessly for the development 
of the Masjid, having pure intentions for the Masjid. The committee will
